Who is responsive for data treatment

RodDroneGo, S.A, based in Porto, is responsible for collecting and processing personal data. The company has designated a Data Protection Officer, who is responsible for ensuring compliance with the GDPR. To contact the company we suggest to do so via email to geral@robdronego.pt

Why is my data collected and how is it going to be used

RobDroneGo is committed to only processing personal data strictly necessary for the operation of our solution. The personal data we will collect for each process includes:

Client Program RobDroneGo:

- Identification data: name.

- Contact data: mobile number, email, taxpayer number.

These data are collected with the purpose of identifying each of our users and communicating with them in the event of any irregularity with their user account.

Video surveillance and image capture:

- Our robots and drones offer the possibility to record video and capture images inside the campus to ensure its security.

Object request:

- Location data: current location.

These data are collected to be able to calculate the path that any of our robots have to follow in order to deliver your order.

Which are my rights

In general, the person to whom personal data relates has the following rights with regard to data protection: the right to access information, the right to correct incorrect data, the right to request the deletion of data, the right to restrict processing, the right to data portability, the right to object to processing and the right not to be subject to automated decisions. If you have previously consented to the specific processing of your personal data, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, you must send an email with the rights you wish to withdraw to geral@robdronego.pt. After sending, RobDroneGo will analyze your email and assess its relevance, responding in a timely manner. If necessary, the person to whom the personal data relates may also contact the Data Protection Officer.

How long my data is preserved

RobDroneGo only retains personal data for the period strictly necessary to fulfill the services identified above, within legal limits, or until the right to opposition or right to erasure is exercised. Once the defined retention period has expired, RobDroneGo undertakes to eliminate, destroy or anonymize your personal data. To this end, RobDroneGo uses physical or digital destruction methods that guarantee the irreversibility of personal data. RobDroneGo retains personal data as long as the individual is associated with the organization. Regarding video surveillance, we only retain personal data for a period of 30 days, as required by law.
